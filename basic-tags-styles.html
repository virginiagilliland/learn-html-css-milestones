Creating Lists

Html also lets you make lists, both ordered an unordered. For example here is an unordered list:

Ingredients

  • eggs
  • sugar
  • flour

Here is an example of an ordered list, which uses the ol tag instead of ul

Steps to make a cake

  1. Preheat oven
  2. Mix ingredients and make batter
  3. Put mixture in pan
  4. Bake
  5. Let sit and then put on frosting & sprinkles Example of cake recipe

Nesting Lists

You can also nest lists within eachother, for example following our baking example:

  1. Preheat oven
  2. Mix ingredients and make batter
    • eggs
    • sugar
    • flour
  3. Put mixture in pan
  4. Bake
  5. Let sit and then put on frosting & sprinkles Example of cake recipe
